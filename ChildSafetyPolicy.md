# Child Safety Policy for PorosenoCheck Employee App

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

## Introduction

REChain, Inc. ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting children from exploitation and abuse. This Child Safety Policy outlines our commitment to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in connection with the PorosenoCheck Employee App ("App").

## Prohibition of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation

We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation. This includes but is not limited to:

1. Sexual abuse of children
2. Production, distribution, or possession of child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
3. Exploitation of children for sexual purposes
4. Grooming or solicitation of minors for sexual purposes
5. Any other conduct that constitutes child sexual abuse or exploitation under applicable laws

## Compliance with Laws and Regulations

We comply with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including:

1. **United States:**
   - PROTECT Act
   - Child Protection and Obscenity Enforcement Act
   - State-specific child protection laws

2. **European Union:**
   - General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - regarding children's data
   - EU Strategy on the Rights of the Child

3. **International Standards:**
   - UN Convention on the Rights of the Child
   - The Hague Convention on International Child Protection

## Age Restrictions

The PorosenoCheck Employee App is intended for use by adults (18 years and older) who are employed by pet care service providers. The App is not designed for use by children or minors.

## Data Protection for Minors

While our App is intended for adult employees, we recognize the importance of protecting any minor data that may inadvertently be collected:

1. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
2. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information
3. Parents or guardians who believe that we have collected information from their children may contact our Child Safety Officer

## Employee Training

All our employees and contractors receive training on:
1. Recognizing signs of child abuse and exploitation
2. Proper reporting procedures for suspected child abuse
3. Compliance with child protection laws

## Monitoring and Enforcement

We actively monitor for potential violations of this policy and will take appropriate action, including:
1. Removing content that violates this policy
2. Terminating accounts that violate this policy
3. Reporting illegal activity to appropriate authorities

## Policy Updates

We may update this Child Safety Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page.
